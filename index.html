Privacy Policy for Memory Maze

Effective Date: January 17, 2026

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

MindFlash Games ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Memory Maze mobile application (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Game.

By using Memory Maze, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you play Memory Maze, we automatically collect certain information through third-party services:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Advertising ID: Used by AdMob to show relevant ads
  • Game Usage: How you interact with the Game (for advertising purposes)
  • Approximate Location: Based on IP address (for regional ad targeting)

1.2 Information You Provide

Memory Maze does NOT require you to:

  • Create an account
  • Provide your name, email, or personal details
  • Share any personal information

Your game scores are stored locally on your device only and are not shared with us or any third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the Game functionality
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Improve the Game experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in Memory Maze. AdMob may collect and process the following information:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

Ad Personalization: AdMob may use this information to show you personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings.

For more information about how Google uses your data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information

4. Children's Privacy

Memory Maze may be played by children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and comply with applicable laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Important: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The Game:

  • Does not require registration or personal information
  • Does not have social features or chat functionality
  • Only collects anonymous, non-personal data through AdMob

Parents and guardians can control ad personalization for children through their device settings.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. However, please note that:

  • No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure
  • We use commercially acceptable means to protect your data
  • Your game data is stored locally on your device

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

6.2 Data Deletion

Since we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, you can delete all game data by uninstalling the app from your device.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. AdMob, operated by Google, may transfer data internationally as described in their privacy policy.
