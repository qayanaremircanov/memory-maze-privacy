Privacy Policy for Memory Maze

Effective Date: January 18, 2026

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

MindFlash Games ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Memory Maze mobile application (the "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Game.

By using Memory Maze, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you play Memory Maze, we automatically collect certain information through third-party services:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Advertising ID: Used by AdMob to show relevant ads
  • Game Usage Data: How you interact with the Game, including gameplay statistics, session duration, and feature usage
  • Approximate Location: Based on IP address (for regional ad targeting and analytics)
  • App Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics

1.2 Information You Provide

Memory Maze does NOT require you to:

  • Create an account
  • Provide your name, email, or personal details
  • Share any personal information

Your game scores are stored locally on your device only and are not shared with us or any third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the Game functionality
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Analyze game performance and user engagement through Firebase Analytics
  • Improve the Game experience and fix bugs
  • Understand how players use the Game to enhance features
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in Memory Maze. AdMob may collect and process the following information:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

Ad Personalization: AdMob may use this information to show you personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings.

For more information about how Google uses your data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information

3.2 Firebase Analytics

NEW: We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how players use Memory Maze and to improve the Game experience.

What Firebase Analytics Collects:

  • Device Information: Device model, screen size, operating system version
  • App Usage: Which features you use, how long you play, levels completed
  • Performance Data: App crashes, errors, and loading times
  • General Location: Country and city level (not precise location)
  • User Properties: Anonymous identifiers to understand user behavior patterns

What Firebase Analytics Does NOT Collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identification
  • Precise GPS location
  • Contacts, photos, or other sensitive data

How We Use Analytics Data:

  • Understand which game features are most popular
  • Identify and fix crashes or bugs
  • Improve game balance and difficulty levels
  • Make data-driven decisions about new features

Firebase Analytics data is processed by Google and may be transferred internationally. For more information:

  • Firebase Privacy Information
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. Permissions

Memory Maze requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to display ads and send analytics data
  • Network State: To check if you're connected to the internet
  • Ad ID: To show relevant advertisements through AdMob

We do NOT request access to:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Photos or media files
  • Location services (GPS)
  • Phone calls or SMS

5. Children's Privacy

Memory Maze may be played by children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and comply with applicable laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Important: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The Game:

  • Does not require registration or personal information
  • Does not have social features or chat functionality
  • Only collects anonymous, non-personal data through AdMob and Firebase Analytics
  • Does not contain links to external websites (except in this privacy policy)

Parents and guardians can:

  • Control ad personalization through device settings
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the Game
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns at memorymaze2026@gmail.com

6. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • We use secure third-party services (Google Firebase, AdMob) with strong security practices
  • Your game data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store any personal information on our servers

However, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

Local Data: Game progress and scores are stored on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics retains data for up to 14 months. After this period, aggregated data may be retained indefinitely for statistical purposes.

Advertising Data: AdMob retains data according to Google's data retention policies.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

8.2 Opting Out of Analytics

While you cannot completely disable Firebase Analytics within the app, you can:

  • Disable ad personalization (which limits some data collection)
  • Use your device's "Limit Ad Tracking" or similar feature
  • Contact us to request data deletion

8.3 Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  • Local Data: Uninstall the app or clear app data through device settings
  • Analytics Data: Contact us at memorymaze2026@gmail.com to request deletion of analytics data associated with your device

8.4 Your Rights Under GDPR (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at memorymaze2026@gmail.com
